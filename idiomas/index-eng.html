About Us

Family Root Crops

The company has been operating for almost 50 years since its founding.

Back in 1973, it was Don Miguel who decided to open his own path in agriculture, influenced by his origins where agriculture was also the basis of the family economy and already a way of life.

His legacy is carried on by his son and later by his grandchildren.

The main objective has always been to improve our production, ensuring an excellent quality at the service of our customers.

We have incorporated technology and we have a workforce trained in the best crop practices.

We are present throughout the production chain, ensuring high quality standards are maintained.

We are currently working on the implementation of quality standards to ensure the trust that every customer places in La Mercedes.

Fields

With mountain water

Our fields are located at the foot of Los Andes Mountains, at an average altitude of 1300 meters above sea level, near the cities of Tupungato and Luján de Cuyo, in the province of Mendoza, Argentina.

In a privileged location, with soils rich in nutrients, melt water from the mountain range, ideal climatic conditions, pure and fresh air, which favors the production of excellent products.

We have about 270 cultivable hectares with drip irrigation, making it more efficient and also helping the environment in water consumption.

Plant

We have our own production plant located near the city of Luján de Cuyo, Mendoza, Argentina.

It is strategically located in the middle of the bioceanic corridor linking the ports of Buenos Aires and Santiago de Chile.

We have the technology and process control that allow us to work with fresh products, process and pack them according to our customers' requirements.

We have athermal facilities for the correct storage of the products.
